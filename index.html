What is M SNAKE?

M SNAKE is a volunteer-run local math competition made for middle-school students.

Many competitions in the local area are designed to challenge the most experienced of competitiors. However, for novice students, these events can be very discouraging, given their difficulty and harsh competition. M SNAKE is a competition designed with this in mind, and aims to provide an encouraging introduction to the competitive-math world. It's main goal is to foster an environment that welcomes students considering exploring STEM and to give them an experience that could kickstart their enthusiasm for it.

We hope to attract students with little prior competition experience and give them an opportunity to meet new friends, get excited about math, and have fun solving problems with their peers.
